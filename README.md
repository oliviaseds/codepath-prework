## App Brainstorming

- Goodnotes: personalizable organization, compatible cross-device, AI handwriting replication
- Strava: location accuracy, tracks multiple health statistics, can add others to activities
- Pinterest: clean aesthetic, personalizable, relevance of recommended content, privacy options

Chore Chart - allows roommates to coordinate weekly household chores by adding tasks and assigning themselves to the jobs. Dashboard displays daily and weekly chores (each with an assigned roommate and completion status), group responsibilities, and a section for notes/images. Roommates can toggle the frequency of group reminders to complete chores and personalize the dashboard theme.

Bullet Journal - replicates the analog mindfulness practice in an app format. Users can add daily bullet, priority, to-do, event, and misc. notes. Supports monthly, weekly, and daily reflections and mindful prompts with clean app aesthetic in the spirit of the familiar bullet journaling practice.

Media Tracker - users can create and organize shelves of book and movie reviews, including an image, star rating, and note. Includes to-read/watch section and recommendations based on highly rated media.
